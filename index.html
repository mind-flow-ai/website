Grid operators are running out of time

Day-ahead energy markets have fixed deadlines. As renewables grow, operators need to evaluate hundreds of scenarios — but current solvers can't keep up.

⏱

Rigid Market Deadlines

Our Approach

ML-guided optimization, not ML-replaced optimization

We don't replace the solver — we make it faster. Our three-stage pipeline uses ML to intelligently reduce problem size while the solver guarantees feasibility.

01

GNN Commitment Prediction

A graph neural network predicts generator on/off states with calibrated confidence scores. High-confidence predictions are clamped as fixed variables — dramatically reducing the binary search space.

GATv2 + Monte Carlo Dropout
02

Congestion-Aware Line Screening

A learned model predicts which transmission lines will bind. ~89% of 3,200+ constraints are safely deactivated, with lazy re-injection if violations are detected.

PTDF-Based Screening
03

Reduced MIP Solve

The massively reduced problem is solved by a standard MIP solver. Feasibility is guaranteed. Uncertain variables remain free. The solver always has the final word.

HiGHS / Gurobi / CPLEX
